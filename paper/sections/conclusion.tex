\section{Conclusion}

We introduced DW-Bench, the first benchmark for evaluating LLM
capabilities on data warehouse graph topology reasoning. Across five
datasets (four real-world, one synthetic), two frontier models (Gemini~2.5
Flash and DeepSeek-V3), and four baselines, our evaluation reveals a
clear hierarchy: while Flat Text baselines dominate easy local queries,
they fail catastrophically on hard structural tasks that require graph
traversal. Graph-Augmented baselines improve hard-task performance
significantly, but subtypes requiring deep structural reasoning---such as
\texttt{hop\_count}, \texttt{count}, and \texttt{combined\_impact}---remain
effectively unsolved by all methods, establishing a compelling frontier
for GNN-augmented LLM architectures.

DW-Bench is publicly available at
\url{https://github.com/AJamal27891/dw-bench} with all datasets,
evaluation code, and baseline implementations.
