\section{Conclusion}

We introduced DW-Bench, the first benchmark for evaluating LLM
capabilities on data warehouse graph topology reasoning. Our evaluation
reveals a clear hierarchy: while Flat Text baselines dominate easy local
queries, they fail catastrophically on hard structural tasks that require
graph traversal. Graph-Augmented baselines improve hard-task performance
significantly, but the hardest subtype --- combined impact analysis ---
remains unsolved by all methods, establishing a compelling frontier for
GNN-augmented LLM architectures.

DW-Bench is publicly available at
\url{https://github.com/[repo]/dw-bench} with all datasets, evaluation
code, and baseline implementations.
