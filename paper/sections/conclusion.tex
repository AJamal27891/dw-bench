\section{Conclusion}

DW-Bench evaluates LLMs on graph topology reasoning over data warehouse
schemas. Our six-baseline evaluation yields four insights:
(1)~Tool-Use dominates (89--90\% vs.\ 69--77\% static);
(2)~easy subtypes create a Triviality Illusion in micro-EM;
(3)~a hard-question ceiling persists at $\sim$60\% even with tools
(vs.\ $>$97\% Oracle); and
(4)~tools are essential for value-level questions yet insufficient for
compositional reasoning. The gap between tool-augmented LLMs and Oracle
on hard subtypes motivates hybrid architectures combining interactive
tools with learned graph representations.
